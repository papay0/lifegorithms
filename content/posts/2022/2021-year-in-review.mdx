It was time for me to go back to the country I'm trying to call my new home. To go back it was a crazy story about VISA. Covid was still a thing, I had to write a letter to the US government to explain why I'm special, why me being in the US territory would benefit the US economy. My argument was that I was a Lead Engineer at UberEats and without UberEats, Americans wouldn't be able to receive their groceries, and being in the same timezone would be better for me to manage all my projects.
Therefore I need to be in the US to save the US economy. LOL it worked, so they have me a special pass to cross the border, that was pretty stressful. Once the pass received, I had max 30 days to cross the border, otherwise it would expire!

After a few days in Paris, I was back in SF, visiting some places and fell in love with my new apartment in [Jasper](https://www.rentjasper.com/)!
