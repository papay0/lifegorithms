## What's the worst-case scenario?

This question helps me a lot! If the worst-case scenario is okay, then the full situation will be okay, no need to stress or worry!

Let‚Äôs take the example of going on a date, which could be a stressful situation.

What are the possible outcomes?

- I don‚Äôt have a good time, it‚Äôs a waste of time for both of us
- Have an okay time, I get to know someone new but I won‚Äôt see this person ever again
- Have a good time, no romantic chemistry, but I do have a new friend yaaay!
- Have a very good time, I want to see this person again, who knows what will happen üôÇ

What's the worst-case scenario? "Waste of time for both of us". Yes, that sucks, but is it really that bad? As long as I'm okay & aware that this situation might happen, then it's okay! The worst-case scenario, by definition: is the worst and nothing worst can happen. So if I'm okay with this scenario it's amazing, everything else will be better, such good news! I might have a new friend or more!
